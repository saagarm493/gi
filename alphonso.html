Maharashtra - Alphonso Mango

Alphonso Mango, also known as the "King of Mangoes," is a premium mango variety known for its sweet, luscious taste, rich aroma, and buttery texture. It is primarily grown in the Ratnagiri, Devgad, and Konkan regions of Maharashtra, India, and is internationally recognized for its exquisite flavor and quality.
